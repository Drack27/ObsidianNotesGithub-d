# Matthew Davis

**Age:** 40
**Residence:** Not a permanent Roselake resident — stays in town periodically during research visits
**Occupation:** Anthropological archaeologist / archivist
**Status:** Active player character. Introduced [[Session 1]].

---

## Appearance

A man of 40 with a graying beard and the careful, observant manner of someone whose work requires him to notice things that other people walk past. He moves deliberately. Currently recovering from significant injuries: puncture wounds on his back, a bandaged arm. He is limping noticeably as of March 16, 1981, but mobile.

---

## Background

Matthew Davis is an anthropological archaeologist — someone who studies people through the objects and spaces they leave behind. He has come to Roselake on a recurring basis to pursue local artifacts and study the material culture of the region's earlier inhabitants. His academic interests have made him a reliable customer of [[Agatha Graves]], who sources unusual local finds for his research.

He attended the first "Roselake Ghost Hunters Club" meeting on the evening of March 14, 1981, responding to flyers posted around town. The meeting, at the [[The Old Radio Tower|Old Radio Tower]] on [[Mount Roselake]], turned out to be empty — but Thomas McMullin's full case files were laid out in the basement, and Matthew examined them before leaving. On his way back down the hill, he encountered the [[Anglerfish]] and barely survived the attack.

---

## Known Connections

**[[Agatha Graves]]** — A working relationship that predates the investigation. Agatha has sourced local artifacts for Matthew's academic research. As of March 16, 1981, they are actively investigating together.

**[[Thomas McMullin]]** — Did not know Thomas personally, but attended his Ghost Club and examined his files. On his way down from the tower the night Thomas died, Matthew encountered the Anglerfish using Thomas's skin as a lure.

**Fire Chief Bill** (see [[Roselake Fire Station]]) — The fire chief was among those who helped patch Matthew up after the attack on March 14. Matthew recruited him to accompany the daytime investigation on March 16.

---

## Current Situation

As of March 16, 1981, Matthew is two days out from the Anglerfish attack. He is recovering but still in pain — his wounds are visible, his arm is bandaged, and walking is an effort. He has teamed up with Agatha Graves to investigate the creature and the broader mystery.

During the March 16 investigation, Matthew located the attack site at the base of Mount Roselake and identified the creature's unusual tracks. He later returned with Agatha at night and spotted, through a hard intelligence roll, that the folders in Thomas's basement had been disturbed — since identified as the result of Agatha's own imperfect reshuffling during her Session 2 visit.

- **Flashlight** — Carried in his pack. Used during the evening investigation at the radio tower.
