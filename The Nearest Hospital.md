# The Nearest Hospital

**Type:** Location (Absent)
**Location:** Hours away from Roselake by car.

---

## Description

The Nearest Hospital is a location defined entirely by its absence. Roselake does not have a hospital. It does not have a clinic. It does not have a doctor's office. The [[Roselake Fire Station]] handles medical emergencies with first aid kits and a radio for calling county services, but real medical care — surgery, trauma treatment, serious illness — requires a drive of several hours to the nearest hospital.

This fact weighs on the minds of Roselake's residents more than almost anything else. Every parent, every farmer, every elderly person in town carries the quiet awareness that if something goes seriously wrong — a heart attack, a farming accident, a complicated birth — the distance to help could be the distance between life and death.

---

## Supernatural Relevance

The absence of the hospital is a primary driver of [[The Secondary Entities|The Unprotected]] — the fear of being beyond help. It also feeds [[The Secondary Entities|The Isolation]], reinforcing the town's sense of being cut off from the rest of the world. The hospital's absence is not supernatural in itself, but it is one of the structural conditions that makes Roselake fertile ground for the Entities.
