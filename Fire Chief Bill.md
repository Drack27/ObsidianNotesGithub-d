# Fire Chief Bill

**Last Name:** Unknown
**Residence:** Roselake, Nebraska
**Occupation:** Fire Chief, [[Roselake Fire Station]]
**Status:** Named NPC. Introduced [[Session 3]].

---

## Appearance

A man with a prominent handlebar mustache. He wears full gear when responding to calls. In demeanor he is gruff and practical — the kind of person who does what needs doing without much fuss, but who chews on things before committing.

---

## Background

Bill is the Fire Chief of Roselake's small fire station, one of only three staff. Because Roselake has no hospital and no ambulance service, the fire department covers all emergency functions not handled by the Sheriff's office — which means Bill and his crew are the closest thing the town has to paramedics.

On the night of March 14, 1981, Bill was among those who responded to assist [[Matthew Davis]] after the [[Anglerfish]] attack. He helped patch Matthew up.

The following morning (March 15), Bill took some of his crew back to [[Mount Roselake]] and searched the base of the hill. They found nothing.

---

## Known Connections

**[[Matthew Davis]]** — Bill helped treat Matthew after the Anglerfish attack. On March 16, Matthew recruited him to accompany the daytime investigation of the attack site at Mount Roselake.

**[[Agatha Graves]]** — He recognized her by reputation when she arrived at the fire station.

---

## Current Situation

On March 16, 1981, Bill accompanied Matthew and Agatha to Mount Roselake for a daytime investigation of the Anglerfish attack site. He observed Matthew locate the attack site and identify unusual creature tracks — remaining skeptical but deferring to Matthew's archaeological expertise. When asked to join a nighttime stakeout, he declined: he has a town to watch over and only three staff total.

His level of awareness: he knows Matthew was attacked by an animal he cannot identify. He does not yet know the full scope of what is happening in Roselake.
