# Fire Chief Bill

**Last Name:** Unknown
**Residence:** Roselake, Nebraska
**Occupation:** Fire Chief, [[Roselake Fire Station]]
**Status:** Named NPC. Introduced [[Session 3]].

---

## Appearance

A man with a prominent handlebar mustache. He wears full gear when responding to calls. In demeanor he is gruff and practical — the kind of person who does what needs doing without much fuss, but who chews on things before committing.

---

## Background

Bill is the Fire Chief of Roselake's small fire station. Because Roselake has no hospital and no ambulance service, the fire department covers all emergency functions not handled by the Sheriff's office — which means Bill and his crew are the closest thing the town has to paramedics. His known staff includes:

- **Shawna** (also called "Susan" in passing) — the older, gruff female dispatcher who handles most of the medical care; cigarette perpetually in hand
- **Leonard** — a lanky firefighter; assisted with Matthew's discharge and physical therapy
- **Steven** — a sandy-haired young man, late teens or early twenties; helps with evening coverage

On the night of March 14, 1981, Bill was among those who responded to assist [[Matthew Davis]] after the [[Anglerfish]] attack. He helped patch Matthew up.

The following morning (March 15), Bill took some of his crew back to [[Mount Roselake]] and searched the base of the hill. They found nothing.

---

## Known Connections

**[[Matthew Davis]]** — Bill helped treat Matthew after the Anglerfish attack. On March 16, Matthew recruited him to accompany the daytime investigation of the attack site at Mount Roselake.

**[[Agatha Graves]]** — He recognized her by reputation when she arrived at the fire station.

---

## Current Situation

After the radio tower accident on March 17, Bill and Leonard rigged a specialized walker (interfaced with Matthew's back brace) and transported Matthew home on **March 23, 1981**. They began daily physical therapy visits — walking Matthew back and forth across his bedroom — and arranged a supply of MREs from [[Joe's Superstore]].

Around **March 27**, Bill failed to appear for a scheduled session. When he called Matthew the following morning, his voice was confused. He had **no memory of discharging Matthew from the fire station, no memory of the home setup, and no memory of the previous physical therapy sessions**. He arrived with Leonard, who also had no memory of any of it. Both men confirmed Matthew's physical condition matched his account, but insisted they had not been to his house.

Bill's working theory: the spinal injury caused neurological damage affecting Matthew's memory, not theirs. He declined to involve the sheriff, mentioned he had spoken to Susan (Shawna) about keeping an eye on Matthew, and suggested a specialist from Omaha could be brought in if things didn't stabilize. He continued the physical therapy visits regardless.

Beginning around **March 30**, Bill — and the rest of the fire station — stopped showing up entirely. The fire station's phone also went to a dead tone. The reason for this is unknown.

His level of awareness: he knows Matthew was attacked by an unidentified animal on March 14. He does not know the full scope of what is happening in Roselake, and he cannot explain the memory gap around Matthew's care.
