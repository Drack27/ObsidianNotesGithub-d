# Session 1

**Date (in-game):** March 14, 1981, evening
**Player Character:** Matthew Davis

---

## Events

### The Ghost Hunters Club

Matthew Davis arrived at the [[The Old Radio Tower|Old Radio Tower]] on [[Mount Roselake]] at 8:00 PM, responding to an advertisement for the first meeting of a "Roselake Ghost Hunters Club." He made his way to the basement of the abandoned radio station — the space that had served as [[Thomas McMullin]]'s personal headquarters.

The basement was empty. No one else had come. But Thomas McMullin's case files were there — spread across the table and pinned to the corkboard on the wall. Photographs, index cards, newspaper clippings, handwritten notes, manila folders held shut with rubber bands. Everything Thomas had compiled on the mysteries of Roselake, left behind as if he'd stepped out and never come back.

Matthew examined the materials and then left.

### The Anglerfish Attack

On his way back down from Mount Roselake, Matthew saw something in the darkness — a figure that looked like Thomas McMullin. The [[Anglerfish]] had killed Thomas earlier that day and was using his skin as a lure.

Matthew approached. The Anglerfish attacked.

Matthew barely escaped with his life. He fled back toward town, wounded and terrified.

### Aftermath

Matthew made it back to Roselake, where he was patched up by a helpful couple and the [[Roselake Fire Station|fire department]]. His injuries were treated as best they could be with the limited medical resources available.

He went home and fell asleep.

---

## Significance

This session established that Thomas McMullin is dead — killed by the Anglerfish on March 14, 1981 — and that his case files are now available for discovery. Matthew Davis is the first person to encounter both the files and the Anglerfish, and the first to survive an attack.

The campaign is now active. All of Thomas's cases remain open and unresolved.
