# Mount Roselake

A hill approximately 3.5 miles from [[Town Center]], on the far side of [[Rose Lake]], situated between two farms. It is approximately 10–15 feet higher than any other rolling hill in the area — barely a hill by any standard, but enough to earn the ironic nickname "Mount Roselake."

At its summit stands the [[The Old Radio Tower|Old Radio Tower]], the abandoned KGMR station that served as [[Thomas McMullin]]'s investigation headquarters.

---

## At the Base of the Hill

In the tall grass near the foot of the hill, on the path leading up from the dirt parking area, there is a large patch of flattened and smashed grass. Dark red dried blood coats much of this area. This is the site where the [[Anglerfish]] attacked and killed Thomas McMullin on the night of March 14, 1981.

During [[Session 2]], [[Agatha Graves]] found a **large, thin, enameled tooth with dried blood** in this patch of grass — shed during the attack. She took it with her. The tooth cannot be identified from [[Roselake Library]] resources.

The flattened grass and bloodstains remain visible to any investigator who looks closely.
