# Roselake Fire Station

**Type:** Emergency Services
**Location:** South end of [[Town Center]], Roselake

---

## Description

A converted barn that serves as Roselake's fire station and de facto emergency services hub. It houses a single aging fire engine, a basic first aid station, and a small office. The volunteer fire department consists of about a dozen townspeople who are on call but not always available — farmers have fields to tend, and the grain elevator doesn't stop for emergencies.

Because Roselake has no hospital, no ambulance service, and no dedicated paramedics, the fire station covers all emergency functions not handled by the Sheriff's office. This includes medical emergencies, which are handled with first aid kits, good intentions, and the knowledge that the nearest hospital is hours away. The fire station has a radio for calling county emergency services, but response times from outside are long.

---

## Relevance

The fire station is a reminder of Roselake's vulnerability. The absence of real medical infrastructure feeds directly into [[The Secondary Entities|The Unprotected]] — the pervasive, low-level fear that when something goes truly wrong, help is not coming in time.
