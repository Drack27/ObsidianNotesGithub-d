# The Fields

**Type:** Agricultural Land / Hunting Ground
**Location:** Surrounding [[Town Center]] in all directions
**Case Connection:** [[Anglerfish Thread]]

---

## Description

The fields are the expansive, lonely agricultural tracts that surround Roselake in every direction. Corn, wheat, and sorghum stretch to the horizon across lazily rolling hills. During the day, they are simply farmland — unremarkable, productive, and empty. At night, they become something else.

The fields at night are profoundly dark. There are no streetlights outside the town center. The nearest neighbor's house is a distant point of light — if it's visible at all. The corn, when it's tall, creates corridors of darkness that swallow sound and direction. A person walking into the fields at night can lose their bearings within minutes.

---

## The Anglerfish's Hunting Ground

The fields are where [[Anglerfish|the Anglerfish]] hunts. Its vantablack body is invisible in the darkness, and its bioluminescent lure — a warm, shifting light that appears to drift through the cornrows — is the only thing visible. The lure draws victims deeper into the fields, further from the road, further from their houses, until they are surrounded by darkness and corn and the thing that is waiting for them.

The Anglerfish prefers to hunt on overcast nights when there is no moon. It avoids the roads and the immediate vicinity of occupied farmhouses. Its kills happen in the deep fields, far from witnesses.

---

## Atmosphere

The fields are not supernaturally active during the day. But at night, the sheer emptiness and darkness of the agricultural landscape amplifies every fear that Roselake breeds. The Isolation is strongest here — miles of nothing in every direction, no help within earshot, no light except the stars. The fields are where the town's ambient dread becomes physical.
