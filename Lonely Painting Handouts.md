### THE PEABODY HOUSE — TIMELINE OF EVENTS

#### Compiled by T. McMullin

**BACKGROUND**

The Peabody farm sits ~4 miles SW of town. The Peabody family owned it for generations. Big family — multigenerational, maybe 12 people at its peak. Over the years they all moved away. The last family member left in 1968.

That left **Elias Peabody** alone in the house. He was the family patriarch and served as a pastor in town for a while. Well-known. Well-liked. Lived alone in that house until he died roughly 5 years ago.

After his death, the house went on the market. Three families have lived there since. None of them are there now.

---

**~5 YEARS AGO — Mr. Peabody dies.**

Natural causes, as far as anyone knows. He was old. House goes on the market.

---

**FAMILY #1 — THE JENKINS**

Young family of 5. Moved in not long after the house went up.

- Lived there for several months (exact duration unclear — checking county records).
- At some point, the daughter — **Ida Jenkins**, a kid — went missing. Family reported it to the Sheriff. Search turned up nothing.
- Family stayed in the house after that. Grief. Didn't want to leave.
- Then, one by one, the rest of them stopped showing up. Work. School. Church.
- Eventually someone checked. House was empty.
- Sheriff investigated. **Found nothing.** No signs of forced entry. No blood. Cars were still in the driveway. Personal belongings still inside. Food in the fridge.
- Family never found.

---

**FAMILY #2 — THE CALVINS**

Family of 3, moved in from out of town. Presumably got the house cheap.

- Had a teenage son, **Isaac.**
- Similar pattern. Isaac disappeared first — or at least, stopped being seen around town.
- Parents followed. Neighbors didn't notice for a while because they were new in town and hadn't built strong ties yet.
- Sheriff investigated. **Found nothing.** Again. Same story — belongings left behind, no evidence of foul play. House looked like everyone just walked out and never came back.
- I asked Earl at the diner if he remembered anything about them. He said the kid "gave him the creeps" but couldn't say why. Not helpful.

---

**FAMILY #3 — THE JACKSONS**

Family of 4. Artistic family — they were known around town.

- **David Jackson** — the father. Writer.
- **Grace Jackson** — the mother. Painter.
- **Emily Jackson** — daughter. Musician. Attended the local school.
- **Sara Jackson** — daughter. Sculptor. Also attended local school.

They also had a dog, **Filmore.**

- They moved in and seemed to love the place. Threw a housewarming party. Were active in the community.
- About 4 weeks ago, Emily and Sara stopped showing up to school. Friends asked around. No one had seen any of the Jacksons.
- Someone finally went to the house. Found **Filmore** on the property, half-starved. He'd been surviving on his own for roughly a week.
- Family was gone. All four of them.
- Sheriff investigated. **Found nothing.** For the THIRD TIME.
- Filmore was taken in by a neighbor.
