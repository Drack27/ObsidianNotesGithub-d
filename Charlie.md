# Charlie Haverford

Owner and operator of **Charlie's Quick Stop**, the gas station and convenience store in [[Town Center]].

Charlie Haverford is a middle-aged man, practical and a bit rough around the edges — not particularly talkative, not particularly friendly, but not hostile either. He runs the Quick Stop the way someone runs a business they've owned for a long time: on autopilot, with minimal fuss. He typically arrives around mid-morning.

He owns the building that houses both the Quick Stop and the attached corrugated metal shack where [[Sam Reardon]] runs [[The Convenience and Video Rental Store|Sam's Video Rentals]]. The video rental operation is Sam's — Charlie's involvement is as landlord, not enthusiast.
