# The Farmers

**Type:** Community Group
**Location:** [[The Farms and Homes]], surrounding Roselake

---

## Description

The agricultural workers of Roselake — the families who have worked the rolling hills for generations, growing corn, wheat, and sorghum. They make up the majority of the town's population and are the backbone of its economy.

The farmers are responsible for haphazardly routing their agricultural runoff into [[Rose Lake]], which over the decades has turned it into a stagnant, toxic drainage ditch. At least one of them is responsible for the accidental fertilizer spill that gave the lake its infamous nasty pink film and its name.

The farming community is practical, hardworking, and deeply rooted. They are also isolated — spread across miles of farmland, with long drives into town and long nights with nothing but wind and darkness. This isolation feeds directly into [[The Secondary Entities|The Isolation]]'s power.

## Relevance

Individual farmers may provide information about the missing pig (connected to [[The Antimemetic Killer]]), strange lights in the fields at night (connected to [[Anglerfish Thread|the Anglerfish]]), or the history of the Peabody property (connected to [[Lonely Painting|the Lonely Painting]]).
