# Map Prompts: The Old Radio Tower and Abandoned Station

**Context:** Situated on Mount Roselake, the abandoned station served as the personal lair for Thomas McMullin before his demise.

---

## 1. Exterior
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down exterior TTRPG map of a towering, rusted, 1950s-era lattice steel radio tower ('The Old Radio Tower') standing atop a gently rolling grassy hill ('Mount Roselake') just outside a dead-end agricultural town. At the base of the towering steel structure sits a small, flat-roofed, cinderblock, windowless 'Abandoned Radio Station' building surrounded by a heavily rusted and warped chain-link fence. The surrounding hill is covered in waist-high dead grass swaying in the wind, with scattered, strange, thick black slime or disturbing signs of a struggle near the fence line. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows stretching across the lonely hilltop that suggest isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 2. Interior Layout
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down interior TTRPG battle map of a small, windowless, cinderblock 1950s radio transmission building ('Abandoned Radio Station Lair') converted into the frantic, paranoid hideout of Thomas McMullin. The layout features heavy steel double doors leading into a main room choked with dust and obsolete, rusted 1950s radio broadcasting equipment. However, the space is entirely repurposed: the walls are completely papered over with terrifying amounts of red string, maps, and erratic handwritten notes about 'The Entities'. A filthy cot is pushed into one corner, a small, grimy camp stove sits on an overturned crate, and massive stacks of stolen library books and police case files teeter precariously. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting with deep shadows hiding the terrifying scope of the conspiracies on the walls, suggesting isolation and madness. High resolution, gridless.

## 3. Specific Clue / Important Detail
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, high-quality detailed close-up image of a massive, manic 'conspiracy board' covering the entire peeling cinderblock wall of an abandoned 1950s radio station. The board is a horrifying collage of stolen 1980s police reports, faded newspaper clippings about missing people, terrifying charcoal sketches of a massive 'Anglerfish' creature in the fields, and miles of red yarn connecting the town's tragedies to an overriding dread of 'The Isolation'. Several bloody handprints smear the edges of the maps. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized, with a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—moody lighting, deep shadows, and an atmosphere of absolute dread.
