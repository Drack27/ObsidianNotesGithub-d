# Map Prompt: The Roselake Town Center Map

**Context:** A localized map showing the immediate footprint of the Roselake town proper, highlighting its core infrastructure (or lack thereof), main roads, and notable buildings.

---

## 1. Town Center Overview

**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, top-down town map of a small, dead-end 1980s American agricultural town center named Roselake. The map should be focused closely on a small grid of streets radiating off the main two-lane artery, Mule Deer Road. Key features include a large, boxy, flat-roofed retail building called 'Joe's Superstore', a quaint, slightly worn diner near the road ('Tony's Diner'), a hybrid gas-station/convenience store with a video rental shop bolted clumsily onto its back, a small municipal building combining a post office and mayor's office ('Castle Roselake'), a modest brick Sheriff's station, a fire station, and a small, unpretentious school building ('Roselake Academy'). There are sparse residential homes spreading out from this core. There is no hospital. The town looks deeply isolated, weathered by years of economic decline. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG regional map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows that suggest isolation and a pervasive dread. High resolution, gridless.
