# Map Prompts: Joe's Superstore

**Context:** The largest building in Roselake, a standard retail location providing basic goods and supplies to the town's residents, owned by Joe Mencha.

---

## 1. Exterior
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down exterior TTRPG map of a large, blocky, flat-roofed, corrugated metal retail building ('Joe's Superstore') sitting alone in a small 1980s American agricultural town. A vast, cracked, mostly empty asphalt parking lot spreads out in front of it, peppered with weeds and a few lonely, rusted pickup trucks. The store has large, dirty glass windows and faded, oversized painted signage on the front wall. Around the back is an abandoned loading dock cluttered with broken wooden pallets and a large metal dumpster. The building sits against an expanse of dark, empty fields. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows stretching across the parking lot that suggest isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 2. Interior Layout
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down interior TTRPG battle map of a massive, warehouse-style 1980s general store ('Joe's Superstore'). The vast open space is lined with long, repetitive, industrial metal shelving aisles crammed with dry goods, hardware, basic groceries, and faded promotional displays. The front features a row of old-fashioned checkout registers, while the back corners house a small manager's office (Joe Mencha's office) cluttered with paperwork, a large walk-in cooler, and a dimly lit stockroom filled with towering, precarious stacks of cardboard boxes. The ceiling is high, dotted with flickering, humming fluorescent tubes that fail to illuminate the dark corners. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting with deep shadows lurking down the long, lonely aisles, suggesting isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 3. Specific Clue / Important Detail
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, high-quality detailed close-up image of a cluttered, claustrophobic manager's office in a dusty 1980s warehouse store. The heavy metal desk is covered in ledgers, a heavy black rotary phone, overflowing ashtrays, and bizarre inventory discrepancies scrawled on loose-leaf paper detailing shipments of fertilizer, ammunition, and missing supplies. A single, harsh desk lamp illuminates the mess, casting a long, strange shadow on the wood-paneled wall behind the chair. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized, with a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—moody lighting, deep shadows, and an atmosphere of dread.
