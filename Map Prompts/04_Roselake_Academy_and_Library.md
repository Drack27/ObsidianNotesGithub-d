# Map Prompts: Roselake Academy and Library

**Context:** The town's small educational facility that also houses the public library, where Thomas McMullin used to work.

---

## 1. Exterior
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down exterior TTRPG map of a small, unpretentious, single-story 1960s-era brick school building ('Roselake Academy'). The building features a flat roof, a small asphalt parking lot in the front, a cracked concrete basketball court, and a large, lonely grassy field in the back. One specific wing of the building has a sign that reads 'Public Library'. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows creeping across the empty playground, suggesting isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 2. Interior Layout
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down interior TTRPG battle map of a small, combined 1980s public school and library. The layout should feature a long main hallway lined with dented metal lockers. On one side are a few cramped classrooms with wooden desks facing chalkboards. On the other side, a set of double doors leads into the 'Roselake Library', a dense maze of tall, dusty bookshelves, old wooden study tables, a cramped librarian's checkout desk, and a small, restricted 'archives' back room cluttered with filing cabinets and microfiche machines. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting with deep shadows lurking between the library stacks, suggesting isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 3. Specific Clue / Important Detail
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, high-quality detailed close-up image of a cluttered, claustrophobic librarian's desk in the 1980s. The desk is covered in overdue slips, a heavy beige rotary telephone, an old microfiche machine, and several strange, erratic handwritten sticky notes stuck to the edges of the desk that contradict each other with warnings like "If I go missing, don't trust it." The scene should feel deeply isolated and tense. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized, with a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—moody lighting, deep shadows, and an atmosphere of dread.
