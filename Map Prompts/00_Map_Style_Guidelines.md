# AI Image Generator Prompts for Roselake Maps

These prompts are designed specifically for Google Gemini's integrated Imagen model (nano banana). They all share a consistent, standardized art style instruction to ensure the maps feel cohesive across the entire tabletop RPG campaign.

## Universal Style Guidelines
*Every prompt in the following files should be appended with or heavily influenced by these style instructions to ensure a uniform look.*

**Style Prefix/Suffix:**
> "Create a full-color, high-quality tabletop RPG battle map. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map (similar to forgotten adventures or inkarnate). It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows that suggest isolation and dread. Top-down perspective, gridless."
