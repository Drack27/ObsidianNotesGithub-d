# Map Prompts: Castle Roselake

**Context:** The affectionately nicknamed municipal building that houses both the Post Office and Mayor Lance Winnipeg's Office.

---

## 1. Exterior
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down exterior TTRPG map of a small, blocky, uninspired 1950s-era municipal building ('Castle Roselake') sitting on a lonely street corner in a fading agricultural town. A rusted flagpole stands out front next to an aging mailbox and a small, cracked concrete porch with a wooden sign reading 'Post Office & Mayor'. Behind the building is a small gravel lot with a rusted-out delivery van and an alleyway filled with overgrown weeds and overflowing trash cans. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows stretching across the gravel that suggest isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 2. Interior Layout
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down interior TTRPG battle map of a small 1980s municipal building divided into two halves. The front half is a cramped post office with a long Formica counter, a grid of brass P.O. boxes on the wall, and a back room filled with canvas mailbags and sorting bins. A heavy wooden door leads to the second half: a small, surprisingly tidy but claustrophobic Mayor's office featuring a large oak desk, filing cabinets, framed photos of the town on cheap wood-paneled walls, and a small adjoining bathroom. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting with deep shadows creeping out from the mailroom, suggesting isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 3. Specific Clue / Important Detail
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, high-quality detailed close-up image of a massive pile of unsorted, yellowing mail sitting on an old wooden desk in a dusty, dimly lit 1980s post office back room. The letters are marked 'RETURN TO SENDER' and 'DECEASED', but the names on them seem strange or scratched out, hinting at people being erased from memory. A heavy sense of foreboding hangs in the air. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized, with a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—moody lighting, deep shadows, and an atmosphere of dread.
