# Map Prompts: The Collapsed Farmhouse

**Context:** A long-abandoned farmhouse outside of town with a hidden underground storeroom, connected to the Antimemetic Killer (The Missing Missing) case.

---

## 1. Exterior
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down exterior TTRPG map of a long-abandoned, decaying, late 19th-century farmhouse sitting isolated in a massive, overgrown agricultural field ('The Collapsed Farmhouse'). The wooden structure is heavily dilapidated; sections of the roof have caved in entirely, the porch sags under rot, and shattered windows look like hollow eyes. The yard is choked with tall, dead weeds, a rusted-out 1950s tractor, and a collapsed wooden barn off to the side. A subtle, hidden trapdoor or storm cellar entrance lies half-buried near the back foundation. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows stretching across the lonely, ruined fields that suggest isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 2. Interior Layout
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down interior TTRPG battle map of two distinct levels of a ruined, abandoned farmhouse. The first level is the collapsed ground floor: floorboards are rotted through, revealing dirt below; a ruined kitchen, a living room filled with debris and collapsed roof timbers, and strange, ritualistic symbols hastily scratched into the remaining plaster walls. The second level is a hidden, surprisingly intact underground dirt-floor storeroom beneath the house ('The Antimemetic Killer's Lair'). It contains a large, bloodstained wooden work table covered in arcane ritual components, old ledgers, a scattering of missing persons flyers, a dirty cot in the corner, and a small, foul-smelling pit dug into the earth. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting with deep shadows hiding in the underground dirt, suggesting isolation, madness, and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 3. Specific Clue / Important Detail
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, high-quality detailed close-up image of a filthy, bloodstained wooden work table in a hidden underground dirt storeroom beneath an abandoned farmhouse. The table is littered with horrifying ritual components: dried herbs, rusty knives, a heavy leather-bound ledger filled with a massive list of names methodically crossed out with thick black ink, and a stack of faded Polaroid photos of people whose faces are beginning to physically fade from the film itself. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized, with a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—moody lighting, deep shadows, and an atmosphere of intense dread.
