# Map Prompts: The Convenience and Video Rental Store

**Context:** A hybrid business, a 7-Eleven-style convenience store with a Blockbuster-style video rental store bolted onto the back, owned by Charlie, an eccentric tech enthusiast.

---

## 1. Exterior
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down exterior TTRPG map of an odd, hybrid commercial building in a fading 1980s town. The front is a classic, brightly lit gas station and convenience store with a flat canopy over two old-fashioned gas pumps and a glass storefront plastered with faded soda advertisements. Clumsily bolted onto the back of the building is a windowless, corrugated metal extension with a garish neon sign reading 'VIDEO RENTALS'. The parking lot is cracked asphalt with a single beat-up sedan parked beneath a flickering, buzzing streetlight. A chain-link fence separates the lot from dark, empty fields. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows creeping in from the surrounding dark that suggest isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 2. Interior Layout
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down interior TTRPG battle map of a strange, hybrid 1980s store. The front half is a cramped convenience store layout with harsh, flickering fluorescent lights overhead: aisles of stale snacks, a cooler wall of drinks, a coffee station, and a checkout counter near the front door featuring a massive cash register. Through a bizarre archway in the back wall lies the video rental store addition: it is noticeably dimmer, carpeted in garish geometric patterns, and packed tightly with tall, looming shelving units filled with VHS tapes. There is a small, cluttered back office/repair room for the owner ('Charlie') filled with tangled wires and broken VCR parts. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting with deep shadows lurking between the video shelves, suggesting isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 3. Specific Clue / Important Detail
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, high-quality detailed close-up image of a cluttered, claustrophobic electronics repair workbench in a dusty back room of a 1980s video rental store. The bench is covered in tangled wires, a disassembled VCR, soldering irons, and a stack of unmarked VHS tapes labeled with mysterious, frantic handwriting, reading 'THE BLURRED WOMAN'. A single, harsh desk lamp illuminates the mess. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized, with a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—moody lighting, deep shadows, and an atmosphere of dread.
