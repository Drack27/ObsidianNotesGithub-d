# Map Prompts: Tony's Diner

**Context:** The primary social hub of Roselake, especially for the younger residents, run by Tony Manitole.

---

## 1. Exterior
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down exterior TTRPG map of a classic, slightly worn 1980s American roadside diner ('Tony's Diner') sitting isolated off a two-lane asphalt road. The building features large, slightly grime-streaked glass windows, a faded neon sign, a gravel parking lot with a few scattered pickup trucks and old sedans, and an empty, weed-choked field stretching out behind it. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows that suggest isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 2. Interior Layout
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down interior TTRPG battle map of a classic 1980s American diner ('Tony's Diner'). The layout includes a long linoleum counter with worn vinyl spinning stools, a grid of cracked vinyl booths against the windows, a small jukebox in the corner, a back kitchen area with a flat-top grill, deep fryers, and a walk-in freezer. A small, cluttered back office for the owner is visible near a rear exit door. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting with a flickering fluorescent quality, casting deep shadows in the corners that suggest isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 3. Specific Clue / Important Detail
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, high-quality detailed close-up image of a grimy, cluttered corner booth in a 1980s diner. On the scarred formica table sits a half-eaten slice of cherry pie, a black coffee mug, and a scattered pile of paranoid, handwritten notes filled with erratic conspiracies about an 'Anglerfish' and a 'Radio Tower', scrawled on napkins and ripped notepad paper. The scene should feel deeply isolated and tense. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized, with a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—moody lighting, deep shadows, and an atmosphere of dread.
