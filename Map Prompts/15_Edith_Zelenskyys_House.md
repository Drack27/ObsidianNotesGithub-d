# Map Prompts: Edith Zelenskyy's House

**Context:** The current target of the Mirror Haunt, a creature born of the fear of being watched.

---

## 1. Exterior
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down exterior TTRPG map of a seemingly normal, well-kept, modest 1980s American suburban home ('Edith Zelenskyy's House') located in a fading, isolated agricultural town. A small, neat front lawn with slightly overgrown flower beds sits behind a low picket fence, alongside a single-car driveway containing a beige station wagon. A small, dark backyard contains a modest patio and a child's rusted swingset. The house's windows are drawn tight with heavy curtains, the glass catching strange glints from a flickering streetlamp. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows stretching across the tidy lawn that suggest isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 2. Interior Layout
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down interior TTRPG battle map of a painfully ordinary 1980s single-story suburban home ('Edith Zelenskyy's House'), completely dominated by a terrifying amount of shattered and frantically covered mirrors. The layout includes a living room covered in thick floral carpets, a tiled kitchen, a bathroom, and a small bedroom. The defining feature is the overwhelming presence of heavy blankets, bedsheets, and towels nailed frantically over every reflective surface in the house (mirrors, windows, glass cabinet doors). Shards of broken glass heavily litter the bathroom and hallway floors, reflecting a terrifying, fractured image of the ceiling. A frantic barricade of furniture blocks the bedroom door. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting with terrifyingly deep shadows stretching out from the broken shards of glass, suggesting isolation, madness, and pure supernatural dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 3. Specific Clue / Important Detail
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, high-quality detailed close-up image of a violently shattered vanity mirror in a 1980s suburban bathroom. The mirror shards are embedded deeply in the floral wallpaper, the sink basin is filled with broken glass, and jagged pieces reflect an impossible, terrifying angle of a darkened corner of the room that physically shouldn't exist. There are heavy, desperate scratch marks running down the cracked glass, as if something from the other side tried to claw its way out. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized, with a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—moody lighting, deep shadows, and an atmosphere of absolute dread and paranoia.
