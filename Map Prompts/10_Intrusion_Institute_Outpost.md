# Map Prompts: Intrusion Institute Monitoring Station

**Context:** A covert, tiny outpost operated by a global paranormal containment organization, disguised by the chain-link fence around the toxic Rose Lake.

---

## 1. Exterior
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down exterior TTRPG map of an isolated, suspicious-looking cinderblock structure disguised as a small water treatment facility ('Intrusion Institute Monitoring Station'). It sits on the edge of a large, heavily fenced-off endorheic drainage basin ('Rose Lake') covered in a toxic-looking pink algae film. The area is heavily overgrown with dead, brown weeds. High chain-link fences topped with razor wire, faded biohazard signs, and harsh floodlights surround the small, windowless building. A single dirt track leads to a heavy steel security door. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows stretching across the dead grass that suggest isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 2. Interior Layout
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down interior TTRPG battle map of a covert, high-tech 1980s surveillance bunker hidden inside a cinderblock shell ('Intrusion Institute Monitoring Station'). The layout is stark, metallic, and claustrophobic. It features an airlock entry, a main operations room dominated by banks of glowing green CRT monitors, reel-to-reel tape recorders, and complicated seismograph-like reading equipment tracking supernatural 'Entities'. There are small, spartan living quarters with bunk beds, a heavily reinforced armory holding specialized containment gear, and a small laboratory cluttered with strange organic samples. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting with the harsh, sterile glow of monitors casting long shadows in the tight corridors, suggesting isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 3. Specific Clue / Important Detail
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, high-quality detailed close-up image of a massive bank of glowing green CRT monitors in a dark, claustrophobic 1980s surveillance bunker. The screens display grainy, distorted black-and-white camera feeds of empty fields, the town's quiet streets, and strange, erratic energy readings spiking wildly. On the metal console beneath the screens rests an open manila folder stamped 'TOP SECRET: SECONDARY ENTITY PRESENCE', filled with heavily redacted documents and blurry photos of the 'Mirror Haunt'. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized, with a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—moody lighting, deep shadows, and an atmosphere of paranoia and dread.
