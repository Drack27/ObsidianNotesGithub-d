# Map Prompts: The Sheriff's Office

**Context:** Headquarters for local law enforcement, led by the overworked and pragmatic Sheriff Hank Thompson.

---

## 1. Exterior
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down exterior TTRPG map of a small, blocky, unpretentious brick building serving as the 'Sheriff's Office' in a fading 1980s agricultural town. Out front sits a small asphalt lot with two distinctively marked 1980s sheriff's cruisers parked under a dim streetlamp. A heavy steel door marks the entrance, and there is a small, chain-link enclosed side yard for impound or parking. The building is surrounded by empty, overgrown lots and stands stubbornly against the lonely night. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows creeping across the pavement that suggest isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 2. Interior Layout
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down interior TTRPG battle map of a cramped, heavily worn 1980s Sheriff's station. The front entry features a small reception desk protected by thick security glass, opening into a 'bullpen' with several cluttered metal desks, overflowing file cabinets, and corkboards pinned with missing persons flyers. In the back is a heavy steel door leading to a block of two holding cells with barred fronts, a stark interrogation room with a simple table, an evidence locker crammed with dusty boxes, and a small, messy private office for the Sheriff ('Sheriff Hank Thompson'). The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting with deep shadows hiding in the holding cells, suggesting isolation and a heavy atmosphere of unsolved dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 3. Specific Clue / Important Detail
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, high-quality detailed close-up image of an overflowing, disorganized corkboard in a 1980s Sheriff's office 'bullpen'. The board is heavily pinned with faded, yellowing missing persons flyers, grainy black-and-white photos of empty fields, and red string connecting erratic, seemingly unrelated disappearances, but several faces on the flyers appear strangely blurred or smudged out as if forgotten. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized, with a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—moody lighting, deep shadows, and an atmosphere of dread.
