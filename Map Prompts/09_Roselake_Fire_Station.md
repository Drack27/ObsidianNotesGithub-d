# Map Prompts: Roselake Fire Station

**Context:** The town's fire station, forced to cover all medical emergencies since Roselake has no hospital.

---

## 1. Exterior
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down exterior TTRPG map of a modest, slightly weathered 1960s-era firehouse ('Roselake Fire Station') sitting on a lonely street corner in a fading agricultural town. A large, red, 1980s fire engine sits in one of the open bays, and an old ambulance is parked outside in the cracked concrete driveway. There is a small gravel lot behind the building with a rusted metal training tower. The building sits against an expanse of dark, empty fields. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows stretching across the concrete that suggest isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 2. Interior Layout
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down interior TTRPG battle map of a small, heavily worn 1980s fire station forced to handle medical emergencies. The main feature is a large, high-ceilinged bay housing a classic 1980s fire engine, a heavy, boxy ambulance, and racks of heavy turnout gear along the brick walls. Beside the bays is a cramped, improvised medical triage room filled with dusty first-aid supplies and a single, lonely gurney. In the back are a few small sleeping quarters with unmade cots, a small, worn kitchen with an old refrigerator and coffee pot, a small locker room, and a small office. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting with deep shadows lurking in the corners of the apparatus bay, suggesting isolation and the terrifying absence of a real hospital. High resolution, gridless.

## 3. Specific Clue / Important Detail
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, high-quality detailed close-up image of a cluttered, claustrophobic dispatcher's desk in a dusty 1980s firehouse. The heavy metal desk is covered in emergency logs, a heavy beige rotary telephone, an old CB radio, and several strange, erratic handwritten notes detailing panicked calls from isolated farms about strange figures in the fields, but many logs end abruptly with no resolution. A single, harsh desk lamp illuminates the mess. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized, with a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—moody lighting, deep shadows, and an atmosphere of dread.
