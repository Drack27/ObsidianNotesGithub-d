# Map Prompts: The Peabody House

**Context:** The deeply isolated, oppressive, long-abandoned farmhouse where Elias Peabody lived and where the cursed 'Lonely Painting' resides.

---

## 1. Exterior
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down exterior TTRPG map of an incredibly isolated, weathered, two-story wooden farmhouse ('The Peabody House') sitting far out in a desolate agricultural tract of Roselake. The house is surrounded by an oppressively tall, unkempt perimeter of dead cornstalks, physically isolating it from the rest of the world. The roof sags, the paint is heavily peeled, the wraparound porch is splintered and collapsing, and every single window is heavily boarded up with thick planks, making the house look like a tomb. A rusted, half-buried 1970s sedan sits dead in the overgrown dirt driveway. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows stretching across the lonely cornfields that suggest overwhelming isolation and supernatural dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 2. Interior Layout
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down interior TTRPG battle map of a dark, intensely claustrophobic, abandoned 1980s farmhouse ('The Peabody House'). The layout spans a dusty main floor: a dark entryway, a rotting kitchen littered with ancient canned food, a dining room with overturned heavy wooden furniture, and a long hallway leading to an oppressive, suffocating living room entirely stripped of natural light due to boarded windows. At the end of the living room, resting ominously on an easel amidst thick layers of undisturbed dust and a circle of dead, black mold on the floorboards, is a single, large, framed canvas ('The Lonely Painting'). The air feels unbreathably thick. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting with terrifyingly deep shadows stretching out from the painting, suggesting isolation and pure supernatural dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 3. Specific Clue / Important Detail
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, high-quality detailed close-up image of a massive, incredibly bleak oil painting ('The Lonely Painting') resting on a heavy wooden easel in the center of a pitch-black, dusty, boarded-up farmhouse living room. The painting depicts an impossibly vast, barren, terrifyingly empty grey landscape under an oppressive leaden sky. At the exact center is a tiny, hunched figure entirely consumed by crushing isolation. The paint appears to be physically weeping or sweating a viscous dark substance, staining the floorboards beneath the easel black. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized, with a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—moody lighting, deep shadows, and an atmosphere of absolute, mind-breaking dread.
