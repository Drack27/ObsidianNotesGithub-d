# Map Prompt: The Roselake Area Map

**Context:** The player characters need a broad overview of the entire isolated region of Roselake, Nebraska to understand their geographical isolation and the layout of the lazy rolling hills, Mule Deer Road, and the surrounding agricultural tracts where the "Anglerfish" hunts.

---

## 1. Large Area Overview

**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, top-down area map of an isolated, dead-end 1980s agricultural town called Roselake, Nebraska. The landscape is dominated by lazily rolling hills and vast, lonely agricultural fields stretching to the edges. A single two-lane road, 'Mule Deer Road', cuts straight through the landscape from north to south. Near the center is a small, clustered town footprint with a few notable buildings, looking insignificant against the vastness of the empty fields. To the south of town is a small, heavily fenced-off endorheic drainage basin ('Rose Lake') covered in a toxic-looking pink algae film. Slightly north of town is 'Mount Roselake', a gently rolling hill that is barely taller than the others, crowned with a small abandoned radio tower. A river swoops lazily about a mile to the south of the town boundaries. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG regional map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep shadows stretching across the lonely fields that suggest isolation and dread. High resolution, gridless.
