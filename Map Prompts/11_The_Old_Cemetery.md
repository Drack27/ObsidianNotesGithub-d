# Map Prompts: The Old Cemetery

**Context:** Roselake's original burial ground at the south end of town, dating back to the 1840s, connected to the Blurred Woman case.

---

## 1. Exterior
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down exterior TTRPG map of an incredibly old, overgrown, sprawling graveyard dating back to the 1840s ('The Old Cemetery'), sitting on the south edge of a dead-end agricultural town. The north section is filled with illegible, crumbling, moss-covered tombstones, many unmarked plots covered in wild grass, and twisted, dead oak trees. The newer southern section contains more modern stones but is still unkempt. A rusted, ornate wrought-iron fence surrounds the perimeter, half-swallowed by creeping vines. A small, collapsing stone mausoleum sits near the center, and a narrow dirt path winds aimlessly through the markers. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting, with deep, terrifying shadows stretching across the forgotten graves that suggest isolation and supernatural dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 2. Interior Layout (Mausoleum/Crypts)
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color top-down interior TTRPG battle map of a dark, cramped, decaying 19th-century stone mausoleum in 'The Old Cemetery'. The layout is simple but oppressive: a heavy iron gate opens into a narrow, stone-paved chamber lined with stacked, sealed stone burial niches along the walls. Several of the marble seals are cracked, and one is completely shattered, revealing the dark void behind it. Thick layers of dust and cobwebs cover the ground, and the air feels unnaturally still. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized like a premium modern TTRPG map. It should have a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—slightly muted, moody lighting with deep shadows hiding in the open crypts, suggesting isolation and ancient dread. High resolution, gridless.

## 3. Specific Clue / Important Detail
**Prompt:**
> Generate a full-color, high-quality detailed close-up image of an ancient, severely weathered, illegible 1840s tombstone in an overgrown, dark cemetery. The stone is covered in thick green moss and lichen, completely obscuring the name, but fresh, frantic scratches in the dirt below it reveal half-buried, broken daguerreotype photos of a woman whose face has been completely smudged out. The scene should feel deeply isolated and tense. The art style should be highly detailed, realistic but slightly stylized, with a subtle, eerie 1980s mystery/horror aesthetic—moody lighting, deep shadows, and an atmosphere of dread.
