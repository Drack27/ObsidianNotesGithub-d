## What Is Known

A tiny monitoring station — not a major installation, more like a weather station with better equipment — operated by a global paranormal containment organization called the Intrusion Institute. The chain-link fence around Rose Lake and the lake's toxic reputation provide convenient cover for the station's presence. The operatives are monitoring escalating paranormal activity in the Roselake area.

The Institute's staff are accustomed to the world of espionage and surveillance, which has given [[The Secondary Entities|The Eye]] a foothold in the area despite that entity having little presence among the townspeople themselves.

The Institute intends to keep its existence and purpose a strict secret, because they understand how the Entities work — concentrated fear feeds them, and public knowledge of the supernatural would generate exactly the kind of fear that makes things worse.
