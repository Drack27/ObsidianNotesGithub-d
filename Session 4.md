# Session 4

**Date:** Evening of March 17, 1981
**Character Focus:** Victor Hayes

## Summary
The session follows **Victor Hayes**, a 27-year-old semi-retired FBI agent (European agent) and Roselake native, returning to town amid reports of strange happenings and disappearances.

### Mount Roselake and The Old Radio Tower
Victor's investigation begins around 7:00-8:00 PM at the base of Mount Roselake. He notices trampled grass indicating a scuffle. He creeps to the top of the hill and finds the abandoned radio tower and its concrete basement. Inside the basement, he discovers an organized workspace (desk, typewriter, empty files) and a large corkboard detailing a "Skinwalkers" conspiracy theory.

Victor finds and photographs several key items:
*   The corkboard tying the mayor and other townspeople to the "Skinwalkers".
*   Six detailed case files: The Case of the Peabody House, The Case of the Mirror Haunt, The Case of the Weeping House, The Case of the Missing Missing, The Case of the Blurred Woman, and The Case of the Scratching.
*   A Manila folder labeled "The Skinwalkers" containing a sticky note reading, "Trust it, don't trust me."

He takes all the files with him. In a rusted box, he encounters moths but manages to secure three VHS tapes (dated 1961, 1955, and 1950) before leaving.

### Rose Lake
Around 9:00 PM, Victor drives to Rose Lake, the town's fenced-off drainage ditch known for its toxic algae blooms. Walking the perimeter, he notes:
*   A drainage ditch depositing water filled with nitrate fertilizer.
*   On the far side of the lake, in a sweeping outcropping of the fence line, he spots a tiny concrete building covered in antennas and satellite dishes. This is the **Intrusion Institute's monitoring station**.

He leaves feeling watched but spots nothing following him.

### Joe's Superstore
Victor drives to Joe's Superstore around midnight. He encounters a tired, glassy-eyed checkout clerk and one other farmer. He notes the massive size of the store, combining groceries, farm equipment, and a pharmacy. He purchases a first aid kit to blend in and scopes the area before leaving.

### Charlie's Quick Stop and Sam's Video Rentals
At 1:00 AM, Victor stops at Charlie's Quick Stop. He meets the teenager running the gas station and observes the corrugated metal shack attached to the side serving as Sam's Video Rentals (owned by Charlie).

While investigating the alleys around the store, he spots a massive, bear-sized, vantablack "void" entity absorbing light. Snaking from the front of the entity is a tether connected to the draped, deflated skin of a Caucasian male wearing a tweed blazer and khaki pants. Victor correctly stays hidden and the entity slowly slinks away.

He then investigates the dumpster behind the building and, enduring the stench, successfully recovers:
*   A gunky VHS tape in a case.
*   A gunky VHS tape in a sleeve.
*   A flathead screwdriver.
*   A crud-covered mystery electronic machine.

Victor takes his findings and heads home for the night.
