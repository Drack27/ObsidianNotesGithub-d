# Wendy Simons

**Age:** 32
**Residence:** Roselake, Nebraska
**Occupation:** Cashier, [[Joe's Superstore]] (lane 7)
**Status:** NPC. Skin puppet — canonically replaced by the [[Anglerfish]].

---

## Appearance

A woman in her early thirties. Typically seen in her cashier's apron behind the register at [[Joe's Superstore]]. Since her replacement she has looked washed-out under the store's fluorescent lights — staring straight ahead, neutral expression, no eye contact — like she is sleepwalking through her shift.

---

## Before the Replacement

Before the Anglerfish took her, Wendy was one of the most socially visible people in town. She was warm, chatty, and knew the names of nearly every regular customer at the superstore. She was a steady member of [[Reverend Detty|First Methodist]], attended potlucks, and was well-liked.

## After the Replacement

The thing wearing Wendy's skin runs her existing routines without adaptation. It comes to work on her normal shifts. It scans items, bags them, says "have a nice day," and goes home. It continues to feed her cat. That is all it does. It does not chat, it does not greet regulars by name, it does not deviate from the script. Her coworker Deb has described it, with evident fear, as "the lights are on but nobody's home." (See [[Anglerfish Handouts]] for Thomas's full index-card entry.)

She has stopped attending First Methodist. This is one of the clearest behavior changes to an outside observer and is part of why [[Reverend Detty]] began paying attention to her.

---

## Known Observations

**[[Session 3]] — March 16, 1981 (afternoon):** [[Matthew Davis]] and [[Agatha Graves]] observed her working the register at [[Joe's Superstore]] lane 7. She stared straight ahead, moved items mechanically, and did not respond to the customer she was serving or to anyone nearby. Both Matthew and Agatha recognized her as one of the subjects documented on [[Thomas McMullin]]'s Skinwalkers corkboard.

**[[Session 5]] — March 17, 1981 (morning):** [[Matthew Davis]] visited Joe's Superstore to buy ammunition. Wendy was not working that shift; a scruffy, stubble-faced man was at the register instead. She still holds the job — she simply was not on duty that morning.

**[[Session 5]] — March 17, 1981 (evening):** [[Reverend Detty]], speaking with Matthew on Mount Roselake, noted that Wendy had stopped attending First Methodist about a week earlier and had been stiff and distant the last time he had seen her at the store.

---

## Canonical Truth (GM Reference)

Wendy was genuinely bitten and replaced by the [[Anglerfish]]. She is one of the creature's confirmed skin puppets, per [[Thomas McMullin]]'s [[Anglerfish Handouts|Skinwalkers corkboard]] and the creature's documented M.O. The real Wendy is presumed dead; what goes to her shifts, what feeds her cat, what goes home to her empty house each night is not Wendy.

The player characters do not know she has been replaced. They have only noticed the behavior change and the absence from church.
