## What Is Known

The covert operatives stationed at the tiny monitoring station hidden at Rose Lake. They are actively monitoring the paranormal anomalies in Roselake and keeping their presence — and their knowledge of how the Entities operate — a strict secret. Their professional familiarity with espionage and surveillance has inadvertently given [[The Secondary Entities|The Eye]] a foothold in the area.
