# The Sheriff's Office

**Type:** Law Enforcement
**Location:** [[Town Center]], Roselake
**Staff:** [[The Sheriff|Sheriff Hank Thompson]], one part-time deputy

---

## Description

A small, single-story brick building near the center of town. It contains a front office with a reception desk, the Sheriff's private office, a single holding cell that hasn't been used in months, and a storage room full of case files in varying states of organization. A patrol truck is parked out front — the office's only vehicle.

The building has the energy of a place that is technically operational but fundamentally understaffed. There is one sheriff and one part-time deputy for a town of 651 people spread across miles of farmland. The phones ring. The files pile up. Cases go cold.

---

## Relevance

Players may need to access case files, report incidents, or seek the Sheriff's cooperation. Sheriff Thompson is cooperative but skeptical — he doesn't believe in the supernatural and has no framework for understanding what's happening in his town. His case files on the Peabody house disappearances and Bill Donahue's murder are thin and unhelpful, but they exist.
