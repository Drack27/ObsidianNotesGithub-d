# Reverend Detty

**Full name:** Reverend George "Detty" Ndetti
**Age:** 59
**Residence:** Roselake, Nebraska
**Occupation:** Pastor at First Methodist Church
**Status:** Active player character. Introduced [[Session 5]].

---

## Appearance

A 59-year-old African American man, handsome and well-built, with a bald head and a short goatee or beard. He wears clerical garb — plain black shirt with the white tab collar — even when he is not formally on pastoral duty. He carries himself with steady, deliberate presence.

As of March 17, 1981, his right arm is splinted and bound in a sling. He was given emergency care at the [[Roselake Fire Station]] the night of the fall.

---

## Background

Reverend George Ndetti — "Detty" to most people who know him casually — is the pastor of **First Methodist**, Roselake's principal Methodist congregation. He is the same Reverend whose church shows up in [[Thomas McMullin]]'s Skinwalker notes as the social center for several of the people Thomas had flagged, including [[Wendy Simons]] and the Carmody couple. He has been the shepherd of that small-town congregation for long enough to notice when one of his regulars stops showing up.

Deeper personal background — where he trained, his family, how long he has been at First Methodist — is not yet established.

### Dark Secret

Detty has been staging fake "miracles" around town as a way to bolster his own prestige and the standing of his church. The full extent of this practice — how long it has been going on, who has witnessed what, and how elaborate the fakery has been — is not yet established, and he has not shared it with anyone.

---

## Known Connections

**[[Matthew Davis]]** — Met on the night of March 17, 1981, at the base of [[Mount Roselake]]. The two independently arrived at the [[The Old Radio Tower|Old Radio Tower]] in response to the same Ghost Hunters Club flyer (which Detty had grown uneasy about) and decided to investigate together. Both fell from the radio tower. Detty dragged the unconscious Matthew back down the hill and drove him to the [[Roselake Fire Station]] for emergency care.

**[[Wendy Simons]]** — A parishioner at First Methodist. Previously friendly and chatty at both the register at [[Joe's Superstore]] and at church. Has stopped attending church in the past week and has been behaving stiffly and distantly. Detty had been planning a pastoral house call to check on her when he was sidetracked by the flyer.

---

## Current Situation

**Missing as of approximately March 18–19, 1981.**

After bringing [[Matthew Davis]] to the [[Roselake Fire Station]] on the night of March 17 and having his own broken arm splinted, Reverend Detty disappeared. The fire station dispatcher searched for him in the days following the accident — he was not at his house, not at the church, and not at Tony's Diner. On **Sunday, March 29**, Detty failed to appear at his own church service at First Methodist, leaving the congregation confused and without a pastor. He has not been seen or accounted for since.

His disappearance is unresolved. No one in town appears to be actively investigating it — the dispatcher mentions it with contempt ("that idiot is going to get himself killed with his wild theories"), treating it as a self-inflicted consequence of reckless behavior. The sheriff has not been notified, as far as Matthew knows.

As of the events of [[Session 6]], his whereabouts are entirely unknown.
