# Mayor Lance Winnipeg

**Age:** 37
**Residence:** A modest house near the town center.
**Occupation:** Mayor of Roselake.
**Case Connection:** [[Anglerfish Thread]] (future target)

---

## Description

Lance Winnipeg is — or was — the kindest man in Roselake. Soft-spoken, thoughtful, community-minded. The kind of man who remembers your kid's name and asks about your mother's hip. He ran for mayor not out of ambition but because someone needed to do it and he cared about the town.

---

## Current Behavior

Recently, Lance has been acting out of character. He's become impulsive, loud, and prone to outbursts. He got into a shouting match with a council member in public. He made a snap decision about the water treatment budget that nobody understood. He doesn't seem to think before he speaks anymore.

**This is NOT because he has been replaced by the Anglerfish.** Lance Winnipeg is still himself. His behavioral changes may be the result of stress, sleep deprivation, or the ambient influence of the Entities pressing down on everyone in Roselake. The town is falling apart around him — people are disappearing, families are scared, and he can't fix any of it. The weight of that is cracking his composure.

Thomas McMullin flagged Lance as a confirmed "skinwalker" on his corkboard. Thomas is wrong about this — Lance has not been replaced. But Thomas is right that something is wrong, and he is prophetically correct that the Anglerfish has Lance in its sights.

---

## Future Threat

If nothing intervenes, Mayor Winnipeg is the Anglerfish's next target. His eventual replacement by a skin puppet would be immediately obvious to anyone who knew him — the contrast between his genuine warmth and a puppet's hollow mimicry would be stark. The loss of the mayor would also represent a significant escalation in the Anglerfish's strategy: replacing the town's leadership.
