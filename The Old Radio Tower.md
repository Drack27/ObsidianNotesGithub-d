# The Old Radio Tower

**Type:** Abandoned radio station
**Location:** Summit of [[Mount Roselake]]
**Former Operator:** KGMR (station since relocated closer to Omaha)

---

## Description

A squat, blocky concrete structure partially built into the hillside at the top of Mount Roselake, with a rusted broadcast tower rising behind it. The building has two access points visible from the front: a main door into the top floor and a side door leading directly into the basement. A smashed window faces one side. A rusted generator sits against the exterior wall.

**Top floor:** The disused broadcast room. Chips of concrete flake from the walls. A broken On-Air sign hangs off to one side. Massive rusting radio equipment fills most of the space, tangled with wires that have fallen and pooled across the floor. Cobwebs are thick in the corners. A metal railing at the back leads down to the basement via interior stairs. The floor above has accumulated years of neglect but no particular human presence.

**Basement:** Thomas McMullin's investigation headquarters. Cleaner than the floor above — messy in an organized, investigative way rather than by decay. Includes:
- A wooden desk with a swivel chair pushed back; an old lamp; a spread of manila folders; a typewriter; a plastic crate full of empty folders
- A large 4×3-foot corkboard on the east wall, covered in photographs, index cards, and red yarn — Thomas's full conspiracy board, with a manila folder labeled **The Case of the Skinwalkers** on the ledge beneath
- Half-rotted cardboard boxes in one corner containing old reel-to-reel recording tapes, rusted spare parts, and documents too degraded to read

The basement lights were left on during [[Agatha Graves]]'s visit in [[Session 2]] and were still burning when she and [[Matthew Davis]] returned in [[Session 3]].

---

## History of Use

The station served as [[Thomas McMullin]]'s private investigation headquarters in the period leading up to his death on March 14, 1981. He accumulated case files on six active mysteries in Roselake, a corkboard tracking suspected Anglerfish replacements, and extensive notes, photographs, and interview records.

Thomas was killed by the [[Anglerfish]] at the base of the hill on the night of March 14 while heading up to host the first meeting of the "Roselake Ghost Hunters Club."

---

## Investigator Visits

**[[Session 1]] — March 14, 1981 (evening):** [[Matthew Davis]] arrived for the Ghost Club meeting, found the basement empty, and reviewed Thomas's files before leaving. On his way down, he was attacked by the Anglerfish.

**[[Session 2]] — March 15, 1981 (morning–afternoon):** [[Agatha Graves]] arrived a day late, entered through the ajar front door, and descended to the basement. She reviewed all six case files and the Skinwalkers corkboard, then handled and re-shelved the folders before leaving. She left the basement lights on.

**[[Session 3]] — March 16, 1981 (evening):** Matthew and Agatha returned together. The top floor showed no signs of creature presence. In the basement, Matthew (hard Intelligence success) noticed the folders had been moved since his earlier visit — Agatha confirmed she had re-shelved them during her own visit, but not perfectly. No third party had entered the station.

**[[Session 4]] — March 17, 1981 (evening):** [[Victor Hayes]] arrived to investigate. He went to the basement and recovered **all** of Thomas McMullin's case files and the corkboard contents, effectively clearing the workspace of its investigative materials. He also scavenged three old VHS tapes from the rotting boxes before leaving.
