> [[The Farmers|View The Farmers's full details here]]

> [!info]- Mayor Lance Winnipeg
> Age 37. The kindest man in Roselake — soft-spoken, thoughtful, community-minded. Recently exhibiting uncharacteristic impulsive and loud behavior (NOT from being replaced — he is a future Anglerfish target).
> [[The Mayor|View The Mayor's full details here]]

> [!info]- Sheriff Hank Thompson
> A pragmatic, overworked small-town sheriff who is profoundly out of his depth. Not corrupt or lazy — just exhausted and overwhelmed by cases he can't solve.
> [[The Sheriff|View The Sheriff's full details here]]

> [!info]- The Great Entities 
> Four cosmic infohazards that function like hateful "colors" trying to bleed into reality. They lack consciousness, intent, or will, but manifest when fear concentrates. They include **The Predator** (fight or flight), **The Inevitable** (the crushing weight of dread), **The Uncanny** (the questioning of reality), and their antithesis, **The Void** (the seductive promise of shutdown and nothingness).
> 
> [[The Great Entities|View The Great Entities's full details here]]

> [!info]- The Secondary Entities
> Lesser, localized fear manifestations born from the mixing of the primary Great Entities. Active in Roselake: **The Isolation** (strongest; a mix of all three primary fears), **The Forgotten** (fear of being forgotten), **The Unprotected** (fear of being beyond help), and **The Eye** (fear of being watched).
>
> [[The Secondary Entities|View The Secondary Entities's full details here]]
