> [!info]- Thomas McMullin 
> The former librarian at Roselake High School and a known recluse. He is currently described as "very much dead (or something)." When he was around, he kept a lair inside the abandoned radio station at the top of the town's only hill. He had a deep fascination with conspiracy theories, which he frequently discussed with a select few locals, though he adamantly insisted he never actually believed them.
> [[Thomas McMullin|View Thomas McMullin's full details here]]

> [!info]- Tony Manitole
>  A man in his 50s who owns and operates Tony's Diner. He is friendly, prefers the company of the town's younger crowd, and is one of the very few people who ever managed to hold regular conversations with Thomas McMullin.

> [!info]- Intrusion Institute Agents 
> The covert operatives stationed at the tiny monitoring station hidden at Rose Lake. They are actively monitoring the paranormal anomalies in Roselake and keeping their presence—and their knowledge of how the Entities operate—a strict secret.

> [!info]- The Farmers

> [!info]- The Great Entities 
> Four cosmic infohazards that function like hateful "colors" trying to bleed into reality. They lack consciousness, intent, or will, but manifest when fear concentrates. They include **The Predator** (fight or flight), **The Inevitable** (the crushing weight of dread), **The Uncanny** (the questioning of reality), and their antithesis, **The Void** (the seductive promise of shutdown and nothingness).
> 
> [[The Great Entities|View The Great Entities's full details here]]

> [!info]- The Secondary Entities 
>Lesser, localized fear manifestations born from the mixing of the primary Great Entities. The most prominent in Roselake is **The Isolation** (a mix of all three primary fears), alongside others like **The Forgotten** and **The Unprotected**.
> 
> [[The Secondary Entities|View The Secondary Entities's full details here]]
